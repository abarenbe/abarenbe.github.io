Microeconomics

A collection of interactive models and simulations for Microeconomics students.

Consumer Choice

  • Optimal Choice & MRS

    Visualize the optimal consumption bundle where the budget constraint is tangent to the indifference curve.

  • Income & Substitution Effects

    Decompose the effect of a price change into income and substitution effects (Slutsky decomposition).

  • Labor Supply Derivation

    Deriving the individual labor supply curve from the labor-leisure tradeoff model.

  • Utility Surface (3D)

    Explore the 3D utility function surface to understand indifference curve mapping.

Cost Curves & Market Structure

  • Cost Curves Simulation

    Interactive exploration of Marginal Cost, Average Cost, and Variable Cost relationships.

  • Market Equilibrium

    Simulating market equilibrium interactions.

  • Market Equilibrium with N Firms

    See how changing the number of firms affects market outcomes and individual firm behavior.

Macroeconomics

Interactive models for Macroeconomics.

Labor Market

  • Worker Discipline Model

    Explore the worker's best response function and the employer's profit maximization.

  • Wage Setting Curve Derivation

    Deriving the Wage Setting Curve from the Worker Discipline Model.

  • Labor Market Model

    Interaction of the Wage Setting Curve (WSC) and Price Setting Curve (PSC).

  • Keynesian Cross Practice

    Practice Problems For Keynesian Cross

International Trade

  • Comparative Advantage Simulator

    Simulation of production possibilities and gains from trade.

  • Law of One Price Simulator

    Explore arbitrage and price convergence across markets.

  • Trade & Tariffs Simulator

    Analyze the effects of tariffs on international trade equilibrium.

Fiscal Policy

  • Keynesian Cross

    The multiplier model of aggregate demand.

Ecological Economics

  • Supply, Demand, and Externalities

    Explore how negative production externalities create a wedge between private and social costs, leading to deadweight loss.

Public Finance

  • Tax Incidence & Deadweight Loss

    Analyze how elasticity affects the distribution of tax burden and the resulting deadweight loss.
