Microeconomic Interactives

A collection of interactive models and simulations for Microeconomics students.

Unit 3: Consumer Choice

  • Optimal Choice & MRS

    Visualize the optimal consumption bundle where the budget constraint is tangent to the indifference curve.

  • Income & Substitution Effects

    Decompose the effect of a price change into income and substitution effects (Slutsky decomposition).

  • Labor Supply Derivation

    Deriving the individual labor supply curve from the labor-leisure tradeoff model.

  • Utility Surface (3D)

    Explore the 3D utility function surface to understand indifference curve mapping.

Unit 8: Cost Curves & Market Structure

  • Cost Curves Simulation

    Interactive exploration of Marginal Cost, Average Cost, and Variable Cost relationships.

  • Market Equilibrium

    Simulating market equilibrium interactions.

  • Market Equilibrium with N Firms

    See how changing the number of firms affects market outcomes and individual firm behavior.
