The Great Migration and Urban Transformation

Ellora Derenoncourt used a shift-share instrument study of African American Immigration during the Great Migration to study how Northern cities transformed.

  • Cities with a larger in-migration shock increased investment in Highways.
  • They decreased investment in public transport.
  • Commute times increased as a result.
